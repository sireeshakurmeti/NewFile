About KeyPoint Technologies

KPT is a global leader in innovative language technology offerings for mobile consumers.

Founded in 2004, KeyPoint Technologies is the leader in AI, NLP, input & language technology space. Currently on more than 100 million devices, KPT is a trusted partner to OEMs, Operators, and App Developers for developing intelligent interfaces, engines & input experiences.

KPT provides a thriving multicultural environment for all its employees. The software arm of a multi-billion-dollar global conglomerate KPT’s leadership does not believe in micro management. Every individual is a leader in his own right and is at liberty to share new ideas and innovate.
